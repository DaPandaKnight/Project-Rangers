About FlyDreamAir

Discover who we are, our mission, and how we make flying a dream come true.

Our Story

DreamFlyAir started out with the straightforward notion that each travel ought to feel significant and unique. We've linked individuals all over the world over the years, but we've come to appreciate that connections don't stop when a plane touches down. It thrives on the bonds formed and the experiences exchanged at each takeoff and landing. In order to celebrate that relationship, the DreamFlyAir Rewards System was developed. It is more than simply a programme; it is our method of expressing gratitude to travellers for selecting us. We're creating a community of passengers who feel noticed, respected, and valued by rewarding loyalty. Because when you fly with DreamFlyAir, you're travelling with a family that remembers you, not just an airline.

Our Mission

Our goal is to bring people together by providing them with meaningful incentives and outstanding travel experiences. Our dedication to innovation and customer service is shown in the DreamFlyAir Rewards System, which combines technology and hospitality to make flying more convenient and intimate. Our goal is to make every encounter with customers simple, fulfilling, and open. We're influencing the direction of travel while adhering to our mission of making every traveller feel valued, understood, and appreciated by making investments in digital technologies that improve ease.

Our Promise

Since your trip is the reason we fly, we pledge to always prioritise our passengers. Our dedication to service, safety, and care informs every choice we make. We pledge to safeguard your information, honour your allegiance, and provide openness in all of our dealings. We'll keep up with technological advancements to make every flight more efficient, intelligent, and customised. Above all, we pledge to always earn your trust. Every mile you fly with DreamFlyAir gets you one step closer to something bigger: a community based on thankfulness, camaraderie, and common experiences in the skies.

Our Reward System

At FlyDreamAir, we believe every traveller is a hero in motion. Our SkyPoints Rewards System transforms your loyalty into power — unlocking greater rewards as you ascend through each tier. Just like the Power Rangers, every level brings new strengths, privileges, and experiences that make your journey truly extraordinary.

Bronze Ranger – Under 500 Points

Your adventure begins here. As a Bronze Ranger, you’re part of the FlyDreamAir family, earning SkyPoints with every booking. Enjoy access to exclusive member promotions and earn rewards that set the foundation for your journey ahead.

Silver Ranger – 500 to 1,000 Points

With experience comes strength. Silver Rangers enjoy early access to sales, faster check-in services, and extra comfort perks on selected routes — the perfect balance between value and convenience.

Gold Ranger – 1,000 to 3,000 Points

As a Gold Ranger, your loyalty shines. Enjoy complimentary seat upgrades (subject to availability), bonus SkyPoints on every purchase, and access to our FlyDreamAir Lounges for a more relaxed pre-flight experience.

Platinum Ranger – 3,000 to 6,000 Points

The elite force of the skies. Platinum Rangers receive premium service, priority boarding, and a dedicated support line for personalised assistance — ensuring every journey feels smooth, effortless, and rewarding.

Diamond Ranger – 6,000+ Points

The ultimate status. As a Diamond Ranger, you’ve mastered the skies. Enjoy unlimited lounge access, exclusive event invitations, priority upgrades, and enhanced SkyPoint earning rates across all bookings. Every flight becomes a first-class experience — because legends deserve nothing less.
